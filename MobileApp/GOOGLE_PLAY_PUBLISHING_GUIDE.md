## Create Google Play Developer Account

### Step 1: Registration

1. Go to [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console/signup)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Read and accept Developer Distribution Agreement
4. Pay $25 registration fee (one-time)
5. Complete account details:
   - **Developer name:** Flacron Enterprises
   - **Email address:** support@flacronenterprises.com
   - **Website:** https://flacronenterprises.com
6. Verify email address

**Processing Time:** Usually instant, but can take 24-48 hours

### Step 2: Complete Account Setup

1. Go to **Account Details** in Play Console
2. Add:
   - Contact phone number
   - Business address (if applicable)
   - Developer website
3. Verify identity (if required)

## Submit for Review

### Review Process

After submission:
1. **Pending publication** - Google reviews your app
2. **Review time** - Typically 1-3 days (can be up to 7 days)
3. **Status updates** - Check Play Console for updates

### What Google Reviews

- âœ… Policy compliance
- âœ… Content rating accuracy
- âœ… App functionality
- âœ… Privacy policy
- âœ… Data safety declarations
- âœ… App quality and performance

### Common Rejection Reasons

1. **Misleading content** - App doesn't match description
2. **Privacy policy issues** - Missing or inadequate privacy policy
3. **Broken functionality** - App crashes or doesn't work
4. **Inappropriate content** - Violates content policies
5. **Intellectual property** - Copyright/trademark issues

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Approved

**Check rejection email** for specific reasons.

Common fixes:
1. Update privacy policy to address concerns
2. Fix app functionality issues
3. Update misleading descriptions
4. Provide clearer test instructions
5. Fix data safety declarations

### Slow Review Process

- Normal: 1-3 days
- If longer than 7 days, contact Google Play support

### Can't Upload AAB

**Check:**
- Version code must be higher than previous release
- AAB is properly signed
- Package name matches your app in Play Console
- File size is under limit (150 MB for AAB)

### Data Safety Section Errors

- Review all questions carefully
- Ensure your privacy policy matches declarations
- Provide clear explanations

### Payment/Subscription Issues

- Verify your Google Payments merchant account
- Ensure in-app products are properly configured
- Test with test accounts before going live
